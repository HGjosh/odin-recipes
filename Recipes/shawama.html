Chicken Shawama

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • garlic sauce
  • tomato, cucumber and lettuce
  • pita bread
  • olive oil
  • lemon juice
  • spices

How to prepare Chicken Shawama

  1. Mix marinade
  2. Add chicken to the marinade, seal it and wait for atleast an hour, the longer the better
  3. while waiting for the chiken, you can cut the cucumbers and tomato in to slices
  4. Cook rhe chicken and let it rest for atleast 5 minutes
  5. Get the pita bread and spread garlic sauce on it
  6. Put a leaf of lettuce on it, then chicken, cucumber and tomato and more lattuce
  7. Pour more garlic sauce on top; wrap it and enjoy it.
